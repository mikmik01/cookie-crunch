# Meta Snapshot: Gloo & Harley Dominate Bans, Hanabi & Sun Lead Picks

## Key Findings
### Finding 1
- Gloo and Harley are the absolute top-tier bans, making them highly contested picks if they slip through.
- Gloo boasts a 57.13% Win Rate and an 85.62% Ban Rate, while Harley has a 52.21% Win Rate and an 81.93% Ban Rate, placing them as the two most banned heroes in this data.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 2
- Hanabi is a consistently strong and popular choice, along with Sun and Hilda, offering reliable performance in ranked.
- Hanabi leads in Pick Rate at 3.98% with an excellent 54.12% Win Rate. Sun (1.98% PR, 53.71% WR) and Hilda (1.12% PR, 54.24% WR) also show strong win rates with solid pick rates.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 3
- Sora is highly feared by players, reflected in her significant ban rate, but her actual win rate suggests she might be overvalued.
- Sora has a high Ban Rate of 63.67%, but a comparatively low Win Rate of 49.0%.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 4
- Marcel appears to be a powerful pocket pick, showing a very high win rate despite being picked infrequently.
- Marcel achieves a 53.35% Win Rate with a remarkably low 0.19% Pick Rate, indicating high impact when played.
- Confidence: medium

## Summary
Looking at the current ranked meta, it's clear that teams are heavily investing bans into powerhouses like Gloo and Harley, who maintain exceptional win rates. If these two make it into the draft, they're priority picks for a reason. Meanwhile, heroes like Hanabi, Sun, and Hilda are proving to be consistently reliable picks, offering solid win rates without drawing as many bans. Interestingly, Sora is often banned out of fear, but the data suggests her actual game-winning performance might not fully justify that ban pressure. Keep an eye out for Marcel as a high-impact, low-visibility hero, as his win rate is deceptively strong when he gets picked. The hero pool in this data covers a decent spread of roles, with a good number of Roam, Exp, and Jungle-associated heroes present.
