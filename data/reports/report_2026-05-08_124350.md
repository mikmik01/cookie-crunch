# Current Meta Snapshot: Gloo & Harley Dominate Bans, Hanabi a Priority Pick!

## Key Findings
### Finding 1
- Gloo remains an absolute nightmare in ranked, nearly a must-ban due to his overwhelming presence and high win rate.
- Gloo has the highest ban rate at 85.62% and the top win rate at 57.13%. His pick rate is only 0.51%, indicating he rarely makes it through bans.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 2
- Harley is another highly contested pick, frequently removed from drafts due to his strong performance.
- Harley boasts the second-highest ban rate at 81.93% and maintains a solid 52.21% win rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 3
- Hanabi is a quietly dominant hero, combining a high win rate with the highest pick rate among this pool, making her a very reliable and often-available choice.
- Hanabi has the highest pick rate at 3.98% and a strong 54.12% win rate, with a relatively low ban rate of 14.91%.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 4
- Some heroes like Marcel and Estes show strong win rates but are less frequently picked, often getting banned out before they see play.
- Marcel has a 53.35% win rate but a very low 0.19% pick rate, paired with a 37.13% ban rate. Estes has a 52.21% win rate, a 0.71% pick rate, and a 43.01% ban rate.
- Confidence: medium

### Finding 5
- Sora is a hero that is frequently banned despite having a below-average win rate when picked, suggesting a 'fear ban' or perhaps a high-risk, high-reward playstyle.
- Sora has a high ban rate of 63.67% but a win rate of only 49.0%.
- Confidence: high

## Summary
Alright, Mobile Legends fam, let's break down the current ranked meta! What we're seeing is a draft phase heavily focused on removing absolute powerhouses like Gloo and Harley – they're basically perma-banned. If they slip through, they're high-priority picks for a reason, showing fantastic win rates. On the flip side, heroes like Hanabi are shining as consistent, strong choices, often available and performing extremely well. We've also got some intriguing 'fear bans' like Sora, who gets banned a lot but doesn't necessarily dominate games when she makes it onto the battlefield. Overall, it's a meta where specific hero mastery and adapting your draft based on these top bans can really turn the tide.
