The current EXP lane meta is characterized by powerful fighters and tanks like Gloo, Freya, and Guinevere, who are frequently banned due to their high win rates. For returning players seeking strong, available options, Masha stands out with the highest win rate and minimal bans. Kaja and Khaleed also present excellent, less-contested choices for the EXP lane.
