# Mid Lane Meta: Under-the-Radar Power and Contested Flex Picks

## Data Summary
- Raw rows: 132
- Cleaned rows: 132
- Validation issues: 0

## Key Findings
### Finding 1
- Claim: Gord emerges as a quietly strong option for Mid lane, boasting a high overall win rate despite being largely ignored in drafts.
- Evidence: He holds an impressive 54.84% win rate, but has a very low pick rate of 0.68% and an even lower ban rate of 0.27%, indicating he's an overlooked, high-impact choice.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 2
- Claim: Heroes often seen in Mid or as strong flex picks, like Guinevere and Alice, are highly contested, reflecting their significant impact on games.
- Evidence: Guinevere has a high 50.28% ban rate and a solid 52.31% win rate. Alice, similarly impactful, is banned in 29.01% of games while maintaining a 51.0% win rate. Both heroes maintain strong performance despite their high ban presence.
- Confidence: medium-high

### Finding 3
- Claim: Zetian and Gusion are prominent figures in the Mid lane-associated hero pool, with high pick rates and impressive overall win rates, showing they're reliable and impactful choices.
- Evidence: Zetian commands a 1.98% pick rate with a notable 51.53% win rate and a 17.47% ban rate. Gusion, a classic Mid/Jungle flex, is picked in 2.03% of games, boasts a 50.06% win rate, and has a 16.59% ban rate.
- Confidence: medium-high

### Finding 4
- Claim: Vexana, a staple Mid laner, is quite popular but her overall performance is pretty average compared to other strong picks.
- Evidence: She has a respectable 1.71% pick rate, but her win rate is 49.64%, which is slightly below the overall average win rate of 49.87% and median of 50.02%.
- Confidence: high

## Summary
The current Mid lane meta showcases a compelling mix of under-the-radar power picks and highly contested flex options. Heroes like Gord are quietly dominating with high win rates when given a chance. Meanwhile, versatile heroes such as Guinevere and Alice, who often see play in Mid, are frequently banned, highlighting their perceived threat. Popular picks like Zetian and Gusion are also proving to be highly effective, offering consistent value. However, some traditional Mid laners like Vexana, while popular, might not be giving you the highest edge based on their average win rate.

## Caveats
- The performance statistics (win rate, pick rate, ban rate) provided are hero-level overall metrics and are not specific to a hero's performance when played exclusively in the Mid lane.
- The dataset includes 27 heroes labeled as Mid, but specific lane labels for individual hero performance metrics were not provided, so interpretations are based on common hero roles in MLBB.
