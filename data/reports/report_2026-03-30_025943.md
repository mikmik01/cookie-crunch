# Current Ranked Meta Snapshot: Ban-Worthy Dominators & Consistent Power Picks

## Key Findings
### Finding 1
- The ban phase is absolutely crucial, with several heroes considered 'must-bans' due to their extremely high ban rates. Gloo, Sora, Marcel, and Freya are almost universally removed from play, signifying their overwhelming impact if left unchecked.
- Gloo leads with a 93.0% ban rate, followed by Sora (85.91%), Marcel (77.34%), and Freya (68.67%). All maintain strong win rates when they do manage to get picked.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 2
- Beyond the permabans, certain heroes are consistently delivering high win rates, making them priority picks if available in the draft. Estes, Hanabi, and Hilda stand out with win rates over 53%.
- Estes boasts a 54.31% win rate, Hanabi a 53.34% win rate, and Hilda a 53.06% win rate, all showing strong performance.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 3
- Hanabi is a quietly strong contender, boasting the highest pick rate (3.1%) among the top performers while maintaining a very solid win rate and a remarkably low ban rate (9.14%). This suggests she's a reliable and effective damage dealer that players are confident drafting.
- Hanabi has a 3.1% pick rate and a 53.34% win rate, paired with only a 9.14% ban rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 4
- There's a significant presence of heroes commonly associated with the Exp Lane (4 out of 10) and Roam Lane (3 out of 10) within this set of top performers. This indicates that strong frontline, utility, and initiating heroes are currently highly influential in shaping game outcomes.
- The lane distribution of the top 10 heroes shows 4 labeled as Exp and 3 labeled as Roam.
- Confidence: medium

## Summary
Alright, diving into the current ranked meta, it's crystal clear that the ban phase is the make-or-break moment. Heroes like Gloo, Sora, Marcel, and Freya are practically permabanned; if they slip through, they're almost guaranteed to dictate the game. When those top-tier bans are out, keep an eye on consistently strong picks like Estes, Hanabi, and Hilda – they're putting up fantastic win rates. Hanabi, in particular, is a high-pick, high-win hero who seems to fly under the ban radar. And judging by the archetypes shining through, strong Exp Laners and impactful Roamers are really pushing teams to victory right now.
