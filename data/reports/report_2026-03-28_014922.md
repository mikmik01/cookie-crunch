## Key Findings
### Finding 1
- Claim: Gloo, Sora, Marcel, Freya, and Guinevere are the most contested heroes, consistently banned in a significant majority of matches.
- Evidence: Gloo has a ban rate of 92.48%, Sora 86.06%, Marcel 78.22%, Freya 70.07%, and Guinevere 50.28%. Gloo also maintains a high win rate of 54.89%.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 2
- Claim: Supports and Tanks are performing exceptionally well, with several heroes in these roles achieving high win rates, some with relatively low pick rates.
- Evidence: Lolita (56.81% WR, 0.19% PR), Kaja (56.41% WR, 0.4% PR), Diggie (55.42% WR, 0.25% PR), Estes (54.56% WR, 0.87% PR, 29.23% BR), and Minotaur (53.68% WR, 0.6% PR) all feature in the top win rate heroes.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 3
- Claim: Masha is a highly effective but underutilized hero, boasting the highest win rate across all heroes with a very low presence in games.
- Evidence: Masha has a win rate of 57.41% but a pick rate of only 0.15% and a ban rate of 0.55%.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 4
- Claim: Some popular picks like Tigreal and Granger show low win rates despite frequent appearances, suggesting they may be overvalued or less impactful.
- Evidence: Tigreal has a pick rate of 2.28% but a win rate of 45.67%. Granger has a pick rate of 1.84% with a win rate of 42.91%. Both are below the average win rate of 49.87%.
- Confidence: high

## Summary
The current MLBB meta is defined by a dominant ban phase, heavily targeting heroes like Gloo, Sora, Marcel, Freya, and Guinevere due to their perceived strength. Supports and Tanks, particularly Lolita, Kaja, Diggie, and Estes, are demonstrating exceptional performance with high win rates, underscoring their importance. Masha stands out as an overlooked powerhouse with the highest win rate but minimal pick and ban presence. Conversely, some frequently picked heroes like Tigreal and Granger are struggling to convert popularity into wins, indicating potential overvaluation or situational effectiveness.
