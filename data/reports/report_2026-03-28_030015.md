# Finding Your Edge: Underrated Roamers to Refresh Your MLBB Ranked Games

## Key Findings
### Finding 1
- Lolita is a seriously underrated tank roamer who can completely shut down burst damage and projectiles, offering immense utility and defensive power.
- She boasts a high 56.81% win rate, coupled with a very low 0.19% pick rate and a negligible 0.96% ban rate, indicating she's often overlooked despite her strength.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 2
- For those seeking a strong initiator and disruptive force, Khufra remains a fantastic yet underpicked tank roamer that brings significant draft pressure against dash-heavy lineups.
- His 54.03% win rate is impressive, especially considering his low 0.35% pick rate and very low 1.08% ban rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 3
- Minotaur is a quietly strong sustain tank roamer who excels in prolonged team fights with his potent healing and impactful AoE crowd control, often catching opponents off guard.
- He sports a solid 53.68% win rate, alongside a modest 0.6% pick rate and a low 4.31% ban rate, suggesting he's not frequently contested.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 4
- If you want to counter specific high-CC heroes or simply play an aggressive, zone-controlling support, Diggie is a unique roamer with a fantastic win rate who rarely gets picked.
- Diggie holds a high 55.42% win rate with an extremely low 0.25% pick rate. While his 11.88% ban rate is higher than some, it's still relatively low for his disruptive potential.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 5
- Kaja offers a versatile aggressive roamer option with his suppress and damage, making him a great choice for picking off key enemies and providing burst setup.
- He achieves an impressive 56.41% win rate on a low 0.4% pick rate, with a reasonable 6.38% ban rate.
- Confidence: high

## Summary
Feeling stagnant with the current Roam meta? Our data highlights several heroes commonly associated with the Roam role who are quietly performing exceptionally well without being heavily contested. Lolita and Khufra stand out as tank roamers with stellar win rates but incredibly low pick and ban rates, making them prime candidates for refreshing your draft. Diggie and Kaja offer unique, aggressive playstyles with high win potential that are often overlooked, while Minotaur provides reliable sustain and teamfight presence. These picks can give you a significant edge without having to fight for them in bans.
