# Mythical Glory Meta Snapshot: Gloo & Harley Dominate Ban Phase, Hanabi Shines as Priority Pick!

## Key Findings
### Finding 1
- Gloo is an absolute top-tier threat, practically a must-ban in Mythical Glory and above due to his incredibly high win rate when he manages to get through the ban phase.
- Gloo holds an 85.62% ban rate and a 57.13% win rate, despite a low 0.51% pick rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 2
- Harley remains a highly contested hero, consistently removed from drafts, reflecting his strong impact and solid win rate when played.
- Harley has the second-highest ban rate at 81.93% and a respectable 52.21% win rate with a 1.5% pick rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 3
- Hanabi is currently a top-performing and highly popular Marksman, proving to be a reliable and impactful pick for players.
- Hanabi boasts the highest pick rate among the data at 3.98% alongside an impressive 54.12% win rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 4
- Hilda and Sun are quietly strong picks, offering significant win potential when they aren't banned, making them excellent choices to sneak into drafts.
- Hilda has a 54.24% win rate with a 1.12% pick rate and only a 25.38% ban rate. Sun follows closely with a 53.71% win rate, 1.98% pick rate, and 30.15% ban rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 5
- Sora is frequently banned, but her win rate in this dataset suggests that the high ban rate might be driven by perception or specific counterplay rather than raw statistical dominance.
- Sora has a high 63.67% ban rate, but her win rate is 49.0%, which is below the overall average win rate of 52.84%.
- Confidence: medium

### Finding 6
- The current data highlights strong performances from a diverse set of heroes, with a notable presence of heroes commonly associated with the Roam and Exp lanes.
- The lane distribution shows 3 heroes associated with Roam and 3 heroes associated with Exp, contributing significantly to the hero pool analyzed.
- Confidence: high

## Summary
Alright, Mythical Glory players, let's break down the current meta! What we're seeing right now is a highly contested draft phase, dominated by absolute priority bans like Gloo and Harley. Gloo, in particular, is an insane threat if he slips through, boasting an almost unheard-of win rate. On the pick side, Hanabi is absolutely crushing it, showing up as a top-tier Marksman with a high pick rate and fantastic win rate. Keep an eye out for Hilda and Sun too; they're putting up stellar win rates when they get picked, making them quietly strong options. Interestingly, Sora is a frequent ban, but the numbers suggest her win rate isn't quite matching that ban pressure. Overall, there's a good mix of roles making an impact, with several strong Roamers and Exp Laners showing up in the data.
