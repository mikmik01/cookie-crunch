## Summary
The current MLBB meta is heavily influenced by a few extremely dominant heroes like Gloo, who are almost always banned. Beyond these, a diverse range of heroes across different types—including tanks, supports, mages (like Gord), and marksmen—are achieving high win rates. While some popular picks perform well (e.g., Hanabi), others are picked frequently despite below-average win rates. There's also a pool of high-win-rate, low-pick-rate heroes that could be strong situational or underutilized options.
