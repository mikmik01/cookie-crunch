# Current Jungle Meta: Top Contenders & Sleeper Picks

## Key Findings
### Finding 1
- Several potent Jungle-associated heroes are highly contested in drafts, often leading to bans.
- Gloo boasts an exceptional 92.48% ban rate, Sora is at 86.06%, Marcel at 78.22%, and Freya at 70.07%. Guinevere, Helcurt, Yi Sun-shin, and Fredrinn also show significant ban rates of 50.28%, 43.06%, 31.35%, and 27.36% respectively.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 2
- Masha stands out as a quietly strong, underpicked option for the Jungle, boasting a very high win rate with low visibility.
- Masha has the highest win rate in the dataset at 57.41%, combined with a very low pick rate of 0.15% and an almost non-existent ban rate of 0.55%.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 3
- Yi Sun-shin and Gusion remain popular choices for Junglers, offering reliable performance.
- Yi Sun-shin has a 2.08% pick rate and Gusion a 2.03% pick rate. Both maintain a win rate around the average (49.95% for YSS and 50.06% for Gusion). Yi Sun-shin is also frequently banned at 31.35%, indicating his overall threat.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 4
- The data indicates a robust pool of 29 heroes are associated with the Jungle lane, showing diversity in viable picks.
- The lane distribution statistics show 'Jungle' as having 29 associated heroes.
- Confidence: high

## Summary
Looking for Jungle picks? The current meta shows a clear tier of highly contested heroes like Gloo, Sora, Marcel, and Freya, who are almost always banned due to their sheer power, which would undoubtedly extend to the jungle role. However, if you're looking for something off the radar that still performs, Masha is a phenomenal sleeper pick with a super high win rate and almost no ban or pick presence. For more consistent, popular choices, both Yi Sun-shin and Gusion are solid picks, though YSS might see some bans coming his way.
