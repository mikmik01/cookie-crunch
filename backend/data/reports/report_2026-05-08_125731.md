# Current MLBB Meta: Dominant Bans & High-Impact Picks

## Key Findings
### Finding 1
- Gloo remains an absolute nightmare in ranked, nearly guaranteed to be banned out due to its overwhelming win rate.
- Gloo has the highest win rate at 57.13% and an incredibly high ban rate of 85.62%.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 2
- Harley is another priority ban, consistently removed from drafts to prevent his high-impact plays, despite a slightly lower win rate than some other top picks.
- Harley holds the second-highest ban rate at 81.93% with a solid 52.21% win rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 3
- Hanabi is a standout marksman, delivering exceptional performance with a high win rate while being widely available for picks.
- Hanabi boasts a 54.12% win rate and the highest pick rate among these heroes at 3.98%, with a comparatively low ban rate of 14.91%.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 4
- Sora is a highly contested hero, often banned, but actually struggles to convert those picks into wins, making her a potentially misleading ban.
- Sora has the third-highest ban rate at 63.67%, yet her win rate is the lowest in the top 10 at 49.0%.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 5
- Hilda offers excellent value, providing a high win rate with a reasonable pick rate and an accessible ban rate, making her a strong consideration.
- Hilda has a 54.24% win rate and a 1.12% pick rate, while her ban rate is only 25.38%.
- Confidence: medium

### Finding 6
- Marcel is a high-risk, high-reward pick that, when utilized effectively, can absolutely dominate games, despite its rarity.
- Marcel has a 53.35% win rate but an extremely low pick rate of 0.19%, paired with a significant ban rate of 37.13%.
- Confidence: medium

## Summary
Alright, so looking at the current ranked data, it's clear that some heroes are absolutely dominating the draft phase. Gloo and Harley are almost always insta-banned, showing just how much fear they instill. If they slip through, they're practically free wins. On the other hand, heroes like Hanabi are shining as high-impact carries that players can actually get their hands on, delivering impressive win rates without getting constantly banned. We're also seeing some interesting trends with heroes like Sora, who gets banned a lot but doesn't actually perform that well when she's picked, which could be a misplay in draft. Meanwhile, there are quiet powerhouses like Hilda and the super niche but effective Marcel who could give you an edge if you know how to wield them.
