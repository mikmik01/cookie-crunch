# Top Jungle Picks & Contenders in the Current Meta

## Key Findings
### Finding 1
- Several strong junglers are highly contested and frequently banned, making them priority picks if available.
- Helcurt boasts a 43.06% ban rate with a 51.42% win rate and 0.81% pick rate. Yi Sun-shin has a 31.35% ban rate, 49.95% win rate, and a high 2.08% pick rate. Guinevere is also heavily banned at 50.28%, with a 52.31% win rate and 1.55% pick rate. Fredrinn also sees a significant 27.36% ban rate, paired with a 52.47% win rate and 1.02% pick rate. Freya is another highly banned option at 70.07%, sporting a 51.64% win rate and 1.25% pick rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 2
- Masha stands out as a quietly strong jungle option with an incredibly high win rate but very low pick and ban rates, suggesting she's an underrated gem.
- Masha has the highest overall win rate at 57.41% but a minuscule 0.15% pick rate and 0.55% ban rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 3
- Gusion and Sun remain viable and popular assassin/fighter jungle choices, offering solid performance.
- Gusion has a 2.03% pick rate and a 50.06% win rate with a 16.59% ban rate. Sun has a 1.52% pick rate, a 51.78% win rate, and a 9.82% ban rate.
- Confidence: medium

### Finding 4
- While Gloo is technically capable in the jungle and boasts a high win rate, his extreme ban rate makes him virtually unplayable in ranked.
- Gloo has an overwhelming 92.48% ban rate, despite an excellent 54.89% win rate and 0.57% pick rate.
- Confidence: high

## Summary
Looking to dominate the jungle? The meta currently features a few highly contested heroes like Fredrinn, Helcurt, and Yi Sun-shin who are strong power picks if you can snag them. Guinevere and Freya are also top-tier bans who can absolutely stomp from the jungle role. However, if you're looking for a sleeper pick, Masha is showing an incredible win rate despite being almost entirely ignored, making her a high-risk, high-reward option. For reliable performance, Gusion and Sun continue to be solid choices. Just don't expect to play Gloo; he's practically a permanent ban.
