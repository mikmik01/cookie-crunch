# Gloo & Harley Dominate Bans; Hanabi & Hilda Rise in Current MLBB Meta

## Key Findings
### Finding 1
- Gloo continues to be an absolute priority, consistently banned in most drafts due to his overwhelming impact.
- Gloo has the highest ban rate at 85.62% and an impressive win rate of 57.13% when he does get picked.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 2
- Harley remains a highly contested pick, frequently banned to deny his disruptive potential.
- Harley shows the second-highest ban rate at 81.93% and maintains a strong 52.21% win rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 3
- Hanabi is emerging as a dominant marksman, boasting both a high win rate and significant pick rate without attracting heavy ban attention.
- Hanabi has the highest pick rate at 3.98% among the top heroes, paired with a strong 54.12% win rate and a relatively low ban rate of 14.91%.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 4
- Hilda is a quietly strong performer, delivering high win rates as a reliable pick.
- Hilda maintains a robust 54.24% win rate with a moderate pick rate of 1.12% and a ban rate of 25.38%, suggesting she's a solid, less contested option.
- Confidence: medium

### Finding 5
- Estes remains a crucial support, frequently banned to deny teams vital sustain and utility.
- Estes has a significant ban rate of 43.01% and a healthy 52.21% win rate, highlighting his importance in team compositions.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 6
- Sora is a puzzling hero; despite a very high ban rate, her win rate suggests players struggle to consistently utilize her effectively.
- Sora holds a high ban rate of 63.67% but a notably low win rate of 49.0%.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 7
- Marcel is a high-risk, high-reward specialist, showing exceptional performance for those who master him, despite a very low pick rate.
- Marcel boasts a strong 53.35% win rate, yet has an extremely low pick rate of 0.19%, with a decent ban rate of 37.13% indicating his potential impact.
- Confidence: medium

## Summary
Alright, looking at the current ranked meta, it's pretty clear that Gloo and Harley are the top dogs, practically permabanned in most drafts. If you're a drafter, these two are almost certainly going to be off the board. Beyond the ban phase, we're seeing some real powerhouses like Hanabi and Hilda shining through, offering high win rates without attracting the same ban pressure, making them excellent priority picks. Supports like Estes are still highly valued and often banned to deny their team-sustaining capabilities. Interestingly, Sora is a major 'fear ban' despite a struggling win rate, suggesting she's either hard to pilot or teams are overestimating her current raw power. Keep an eye out for niche but strong picks like Marcel too, if you're looking for an off-meta advantage. Overall, the meta seems to reward securing strong early game presence and reliable late-game carries while denying key disruptive heroes.
