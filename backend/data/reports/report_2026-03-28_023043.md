## Key Findings
### Finding 1
- Claim: Several heroes exhibit extremely high win rates with relatively low pick rates, indicating strong potential for players who master them.
- Evidence: Masha boasts the highest win rate at 57.41% with a very low pick rate of 0.15%. Other examples include Lolita (56.81% WR, 0.19% PR), Kaja (56.41% WR, 0.4% PR), and Diggie (55.42% WR, 0.25% PR). Gord also has a high win rate of 54.84% with a pick rate of 0.68%.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 2
- Claim: A distinct group of heroes is consistently banned, suggesting they are considered overpowered or highly disruptive by the player base.
- Evidence: Gloo is almost universally banned with a 92.48% ban rate, despite a moderate 54.89% win rate. Sora (86.06% BR), Marcel (78.22% BR), Freya (70.07% BR), and Guinevere (50.28% BR) also show very high ban rates.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 3
- Claim: Popular heroes do not always correlate with high win rates, with some frequently picked heroes performing below average.
- Evidence: Hanabi is the most picked hero at 2.93% with a good win rate of 53.31%. However, Tigreal (2.28% PR) has a low win rate of 45.67%, and Granger (1.84% PR) has a very low win rate of 42.91%. Conversely, Yi Sun-shin is highly picked (2.08% PR) and also frequently banned (31.35% BR).
- Confidence: high

### Finding 4
- Claim: Support/tank heroes like Estes, Floryn, Minotaur, and Khufra demonstrate strong performance with above-average win rates, indicating their value in team compositions.
- Evidence: Estes has a 54.56% win rate and a 29.23% ban rate. Floryn holds a 54.19% win rate. Minotaur achieves a 53.68% win rate, and Khufra has a 54.03% win rate.
- Confidence: medium

## Summary
The current MLBB meta is characterized by a strong presence of 'sleeper' heroes like Masha, Lolita, Kaja, and Diggie, who deliver high win rates despite low pick rates. Concurrently, a core group of heroes including Gloo, Sora, Marcel, and Freya are consistently banned, highlighting their perceived dominance or disruptive abilities. While some popular picks like Hanabi perform well, other frequently chosen heroes such as Tigreal and Granger struggle with sub-50% win rates. Support/tank heroes, notably Estes, Floryn, Minotaur, and Khufra, are also performing exceptionally well.

## Caveats
- The provided evidence package does not contain specific data filtered by lane (e.g., 'mid lane'). Therefore, this analysis reflects the overall hero meta across all roles and cannot specifically summarize the 'mid lane meta'.
- Win rates, pick rates, and ban rates are general metrics and do not account for player skill, team composition, or specific match situations.
