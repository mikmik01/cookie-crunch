# Current Meta Snapshot: Contested Powerhouses and Underestimated Gems

## Key Findings
### Finding 1
- Gloo and Harley are currently priority bans in ranked, almost guaranteed to be removed from the draft due to their dominant presence.
- Gloo holds an 85.62% ban rate with a 57.13% win rate, while Harley has an 81.93% ban rate with a 52.21% win rate, both classified as SS tier.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 2
- Hanabi is an exceptionally strong pick that often slips through bans, offering high impact for players who utilize her.
- She boasts a 54.12% win rate and a healthy 3.98% pick rate, despite having a relatively low ban rate of 14.91%.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 3
- Hilda and Sun are consistently performing heroes, providing reliable power in the current meta.
- Hilda has a 54.24% win rate and 1.12% pick rate, with a 25.38% ban rate. Sun shows similar strength with a 53.71% win rate, 1.98% pick rate, and 30.15% ban rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 4
- Marcel is a quietly strong hero with a surprisingly high win rate, suggesting he's an underpicked gem.
- Marcel achieves a 53.35% win rate but has a very low 0.19% pick rate, despite a moderate 37.13% ban rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 5
- Sora is highly contested in bans, but his actual in-game performance doesn't quite align with the draft pressure he creates.
- Sora has a high 63.67% ban rate, but only a 49.0% win rate and a 0.7% pick rate, indicating teams might be over-banning or struggling to execute with him.
- Confidence: high

## Summary
Alright, looking at the numbers, the current MLBB meta is pretty clear: certain heroes are absolute draft priorities or ban-magnets. Gloo and Harley are the definition of 'SS tier' and are almost always out of the picture. However, we've got some interesting trends. Hanabi is absolutely crushing it with a high win rate and decent pick rate, yet she's not banned nearly as much as she should be – definitely a hero to consider for your ranked climb! Hilda and Sun continue to be solid, reliable choices with good win rates. And then there's Marcel, a hero with a stellar win rate but barely picked, making him a potential sleeper hit. Interestingly, Sora sees a lot of ban attention, but his win rate suggests he might be more 'feared' than actually 'dominant' when played. This snapshot features a diverse set of roles, including Roam, Exp, Jungle, Gold, and Mid heroes, indicating that no single lane type is overwhelmingly represented in these top performers.
