# EXP Lane Meta: Top Picks and Hidden Gems Revealed!

## Key Findings
### Finding 1
- Sun is a top-tier priority pick for the EXP Lane, bringing significant draft pressure with both high pick and ban rates coupled with a strong win rate.
- Sun boasts a 53.71% win rate, a 1.98% pick rate (highest among the list), and a 30.15% ban rate (third highest overall).
- Confidence: high

### Finding 2
- Hilda is another dominant force in the EXP Lane, consistently winning and often banned, making her a highly contested choice.
- Hilda maintains a 54.24% win rate, a 1.12% pick rate, and a notable 25.38% ban rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 3
- Silvanna offers reliable performance in the EXP Lane, frequently picked and boasting a good win rate without attracting many bans.
- Silvanna has a solid 53.11% win rate and a 1.65% pick rate (second highest), yet only a 3.85% ban rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 4
- Despite an extremely high global ban rate, Sora shows a negative win rate when actually played, suggesting she might be over-banned or is a high-risk, high-reward pick.
- Sora has the highest ban rate at 63.67% but the lowest win rate on this list at 49.0%.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 5
- Masha and Khaleed are quietly strong performers in the EXP Lane, boasting very high win rates despite being significantly underpicked.
- Masha has the highest win rate at 55.06% with only a 0.07% pick rate. Khaleed is close behind with a 54.49% win rate and a 0.27% pick rate.
- Confidence: high

## Summary
Looking at the current EXP Lane meta, we're seeing some clear priority picks like Sun and Hilda who consistently perform and demand respect in the draft. Silvanna is a fantastic, reliable option if you're looking for strong performance without the ban pressure. Interestingly, heroes like Sora are heavily banned but struggle to convert that into wins, while Masha and Khaleed remain under-the-radar powerhouses, ready to dominate if given the chance. It's a mix of contested powerhouses and sleeper picks right now in EXP.
