# Current Meta Snapshot: Gloo & Harley Dominate Bans, Hanabi & Hilda are Ranked Powerhouses

## Key Findings
### Finding 1
- Gloo and Harley are currently the most contested heroes in ranked, acting as top-tier ban priorities due to their significant impact.
- Gloo has the highest ban rate at 85.62% with a 57.13% win rate, while Harley follows closely with an 81.93% ban rate and a 52.21% win rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 2
- Despite high ban rates, when Harley manages to slip through bans, he remains a solid choice with a good win rate.
- Harley maintains a 52.21% win rate with a 1.5% pick rate, even with an 81.93% ban rate.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 3
- Hanabi stands out as a highly picked and consistently strong damage dealer, making her a reliable choice for players.
- Hanabi boasts the highest pick rate at 3.98% among the top heroes, coupled with an excellent 54.12% win rate and a comparatively low ban rate of 14.91%.
- Confidence: high

### Finding 4
- Hilda and Sun are quietly strong picks, offering robust win rates and decent pick rates without drawing the extreme ban attention of the top-tier heroes.
- Hilda has a 54.24% win rate with a 1.12% pick rate (25.38% ban rate), and Sun shows a 53.71% win rate with a 1.98% pick rate (30.15% ban rate).
- Confidence: high

### Finding 5
- Sora is an interesting case, facing a very high ban rate despite having a below-average win rate, suggesting a potential 'frustration ban' or situational value rather than raw power.
- Sora has the third-highest ban rate at 63.67% but records a 49.0% win rate.
- Confidence: medium

### Finding 6
- Marcel presents as a high-potential, underpicked gem with an impressive win rate, possibly indicating a high skill ceiling or niche but powerful role.
- Marcel achieves a 53.35% win rate but has an extremely low pick rate of just 0.19%, despite a moderate 37.13% ban rate.
- Confidence: medium

## Summary
Alright, looking at the data, the ranked meta is heavily influenced by a few top-tier bans: Gloo and Harley are clearly setting the draft stage, almost always out of play due to their overwhelming presence. If they slip through, they're absolute priority picks. Meanwhile, heroes like Hanabi are thriving as consistent, high-impact choices that players are actively picking to great success, without being permabanned. Hilda and Sun also offer strong, reliable performance. A curious point is Sora, who gets banned a lot, but the numbers suggest her win rate isn't quite matching that perception – perhaps a frustration ban or niche counter. And keep an eye on Marcel; with his high win rate and extremely low pick rate, he could be an underrated power pick waiting to be unleashed by skilled players.
